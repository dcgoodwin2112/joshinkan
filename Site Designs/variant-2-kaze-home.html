What we practice

Aikido is a budo, drawn from traditional Japanese arts.

In Aikido we put great focus on movement, balance, posture, and your state of mind, and how this influences your connection with others. We look inwards to make changes in ourselves, so we can lead others to change.

Instruction is focused both on the basic principles of Aikido and their applications in technique. Above all, it is taught at your level of ability.

04 / Begin Start here

Stop by anytime.

If you are new to Aikido, we ask that you join for one month and attend as many classes as you can. You will be paired with an instructor who will work with you on basic movements and how to safely train.

If after the introductory month you wish to continue, we set up your membership. No contracts. $65 a month.
