0.2 / Membership

How to begin.

New to Aikido

Join for one month and attend as many classes as you can. You will be paired with an instructor who will work with you on basic movements and how to safely train. After the introductory month, if you wish to continue, we set up your membership.

Trained before

Please meet with Sensei Whatley about membership. If you are visiting the area and currently train at a dojo, you are most welcome to come visit us and train.
