The art

In Aikido we focus on movement, balance, posture, and your state of mind, and how this influences your connection with others. We look inwards to make changes in ourselves, so we can lead others to change.

The dojo

A 501(c) nonprofit, operating under the guidance of Sensei Jon Whatley. All instructors volunteer their time. All dues maintain the dojo and the practice.

Stop by anytime.

If you are new to Aikido, join for one month and attend as many classes as you can. You will be paired with an instructor who will show you basic movements and how to safely train. After the introductory month, if you wish to continue, we set up your membership. Visitors are always welcome to observe a class.
