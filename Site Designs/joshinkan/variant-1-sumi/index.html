The way of harmony, practiced quietly.

A small nonprofit dojo in Cary, training in the tradition of Fumio Toyoda Shihan since 2020.

合氣道

What we practice

Aikido is a budo, a martial way, drawn from traditional Japanese arts.

We work on movement, balance, posture, and state of mind, and how those qualities shape your connection with a partner. The practice looks outward, but it asks you to look inward first.

How we teach

Instruction meets each student at their level.

Classes focus on the basic principles of Aikido and their applications in technique. New students are paired with an instructor for one month of introductory training before deciding whether to continue.

Begin

Stop by anytime.

If you are new to Aikido, we ask that you join for one month and attend as many classes as you can. You will be paired with an instructor who will work with you on basic movements and how to safely train. After your introductory month, if you wish to continue, we set up your membership. No contracts.
